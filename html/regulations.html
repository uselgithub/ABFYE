Game Rules and Regulations

1. The playing football field will be of size 3.12m * 1.98m.

2. Each team can have at most 3 robots playing on the field. During the match no team members are allowed to touch the robots with their hands. Handling the robots is allowed only when a Game Stoppage or a Timeout occurs.

3. The match will be split into a First Half and Second Half of 2 minutes each. There will be a Half Time break of 1 minute. A 1 minute Overtime will be given if needed.

4. A Game Stoppage may occure when the ball is out of side lines or a goal. Both teams should return to their side and the ball will be reset at the centre.

5. A Game Stoppage may occure when the Referee calls to Pause the game. This may be due to the ball being trapped and remained stationary for around 10 seconds, or when an emergency stop is needed.

6. During a Game Stoppage both teams should stop controlling their robots and remove the robots from the field.

7. After a Game Stoppage the game will resume with the ball at the centre. Both teams should place their robots at their respective side.

8. The Team Captain is responsible for asking the Referee for a technicalTimeoutduring a Game Stoppage. EachTimeoutis about 30 seconds. Each team may call 3 Timeouts in each match.

9. In case of an Overtime, each team can call 1 additional Timeout.

10. During Timeouts both teams are allowed to substitute robots as well as to fix or modify the robots.

11. Sanctions will be given to team for rule violation.
