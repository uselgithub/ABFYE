The Bright Future Cup - Robo-soccer Competition

Deliver effective STEM education via competiion based learning. In the Bright Future Cup, teams of young potential engineers will experience process of designing, building and programing remote controlled robot; Team based competiion also embraces teamwork and collaboration, discourages complacency and raises studentsâ€™ consciousness of the value of good outcomes

STEM education

Paticipants need to design and build their own robot. Through the process, they will be equiped with knowledge of electronic circuit and concepts of robotics. Furthermore, technique on programing of the robot is also introduced.

Team Building

The Bright Future Cup is a team based competiion. Each team compose of 5 members: 3 starting players, 1 bench player and 1 technical support. Team members need to cooperate on problem solving, robot building and competiion.

Design Thinking and Problem Solving

Through the process of building the robot, design thinking problem solving skills of participants are increased.
